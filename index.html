Software engineering

"Software Engineering" is one of the new areas of training IT-specialists in Russia. Software engineering is the science of how to create large, robust software systems for real business. When creating software products, entire teams of professionals work, which must be managed. Having studied software engineering, you will not only become a software systems architect, a high-class programmer, but also learn how to manage projects and teams in the field of information technology.

Economy

The profile "World Economy and International Business" allows students to gain a set of knowledge in the field of national and world economy, international monetary and credit relations, financial and banking activities, taxation, Russian and international legislation and law, marketing, activities of international economic organizations, etc.

Design

Graphic design is today one of the most promising and relevant creative areas of professional activity. Graphic design is present in all areas of human activity: cinema and television, animation and the Internet, printing, advertising, landscape and interior design, etc.

Interesting Facts

Interesting Facts
“In 1932, although there were no senior courses at the institute, students, under the guidance of their teachers, began to carry out work of national economic importance. So the Department of Geophysics performed about 300 analyzes for the projected dam.”
Interesting Facts
“In 1933, the brigade-laboratory system of teaching was recognized as ineffective. The practice of collective offsets has outlived itself. Systematic courses of lectures are being introduced, laboratory work of students on individual assignments has been strengthened.”
Interesting Facts
“At the end of the 1950s, the institute clearly outgrew the scale of the road school, turning into a multidisciplinary technical university. The transformation of SADI into a polytechnic institute is discussed.”
Interesting Facts
“All students are divided into specialties and specializations and are assigned to the relevant profiling departments. All these transformations were aimed at ensuring that teachers and students of the same specialty constituted a single labor collective solving common problems.”

A little about our city

Saratov is a large city and river port in the southeast of Russia, located on the high right bank of the Volga. More than 1.2 million people live in Bolshoi Saratov. Tourists will be interested in seeing the historical center of the city with its ancient buildings, visiting interesting museums or relaxing at one of the many tourist centers located in the vicinity of Saratov, on the banks of the Volga and its tributaries.
